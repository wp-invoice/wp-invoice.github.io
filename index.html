WP-Invoice

Adds robust billing and invoicing functionality to your WordPress site. You can easily create, edit, and send customized and itemized invoices to your customers without leaving your WordPress control panel.

Features

Various Payment gateways

MerchantPlus.com and other Authorize.net Gateways, PayPal, Stripe, InterKassa, 2Checkout, PayPal Pro, USA ePay, Mijireh Checkout

Send notifications

Send notifications to customers with a secured link back to the web invoice. Reminders and customizable invoice and receipt templates.

Manage your business locations

Create as many locations as needed. Use specific location for each invoice you send to your clients.

Download PDF

Easily generate PDF versions of your invoices, receipts and quotes. The generated files can be displayed as links on the secure invoice pages. Choose from four unique PDF layouts

Create checkout forms

Create stand-alone checkout forms on your site. That form can contain a list of Predefined Line Items of your choice, or open items (custom amount entry). Processed payments are visible on the WP-Invoice overview screen on the back-end, alongside regular invoices

Send Quotes

With the Quotes invoice, we have implemented a comment system with which, your client can leave questions/comments to you and you can reply to them until everyone is happy

Export/Import Invoices

Export your invoices in the XML and JSON formats and import data from other WP-Invoice installations

Sales Visualization

Provides a graphic visualization of your sales, filtered by day, week or month

Option to sign invoices

Get a signature from a client who pays your invoices: you ask for a full name and to write a signature before clicking Pay button

Use Internal recurring billing

This looks like a regular invoice, but it is sending automatically via WP-Invoice each set period

How WP-Invoice works

You create an invoice from your WordPress installationâ€™s admin WP-Invoice section or directly from a user profile if WP-CRM plugin is installed.

Use Predefined Line Items They are common services and/or products that you can create once and use in your invoices.

After the invoice is created, you can send it to your client with an email, with a brief invoice description and a unique link.

Your client follows the special link to your site, where their invoice is automatically displayed for them. After reviewing the invoice, your client pays their bill and you are immediately notified.

Client Dashboard shows a list of all invoices for particular logged in user. Also Client Dashboard available for not logged in user, but with the particular link.
